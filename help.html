What about hashes?

Hashes represent how exactly the topic was explained or done.
Hashes are put in if requested or automatically if seen necessary.

 

Hashes include:

 

Intensive - Content went in-depth.

Quick - Explained in three minutes or less (video material only).

Document - Was not made into an interactive quiz (practice material only).

Decrepit - Content is lazy and will be overwritten once better material for this topic is sent in.

How can I make an interactive quiz?

That is cool you want to join this process!
We believe that all interactive quizes on this site should be walkthrough problems than the traditional manner. We want fellow students to clearly learn from mistakes, and not see a final score to evaluate comprehension. Click here to see an example.


Anyways, you must know it is more work and you will still have to make a traditional worksheet and video to be eligible.

Make a document and include questions on solving one or two problems, step by step with the formula/process you set. Highlight the correct response in the document. Include custom images with each problem. Include a response to each question if student answers incorrectly. Minimum/Max of ten walkthrough-questions. Send a separate link to the document in the request email.

Remember this process is manually verified and done, it will take time before viewed in vault.

How can I remove all of my content from the vault?

Sad to see you go but we respect your decision and will remove you from both vaults on the site ASAP.

Send an email to us (with the same email you used before) saying Erase my content with your full name and each topic you have done.
